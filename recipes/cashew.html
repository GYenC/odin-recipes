Vegan Mango Cream Cheesecake

Description

A recipe originally taken from a vegan recipe book, then alterations were made. This ended up tasting like what I imagine a sweetened suet ball to taste like

So this ended up not having any mango, cream, cheese, or cake in it. I think it stayed vegan though?

Ingredients

  • 5 tonnes cashew
  • Coconut oil
  • Sugar
  • Coconut chips

Steps

  1. Mash 2/3 the cashew together with some of the coconut oil and sugar
  2. Mix the rest of the cashew and oil together until slightly more cashewy than before
  3. Layer cashewy cashew layer on top of the less cashewy cashew-oil mix
  4. Put in fridge to set
  5. Sprinkle coconut chips on top
