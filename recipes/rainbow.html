Rainbow Cake

Description

A moist yet light monstrosity of a cake. Don't judge a book by its cover.

This is actually from a packet mix but somehow master chefs Z and A managed to do this.

Ingredients

  • Betty Crocker Rainbow Chip Party Cake Mix
  • Like 3 rolls of baking parchment
  • Eggs (at least 1)
  • Water, Evian if you're fancy
  • Oil, preferably not motor
  • Icing (some)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200? Degrees celsius.
  2. Put cake mix, eggs, water, and oil in a bowl. Mix until smooth.
  3. Stuff as much baking parchment into 2 cake tins as you can.
  4. Pour cake batter into tins.
  5. Bake until toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cake to cool for 2 hours.
  7. Smear icing on cake.
  8. Enjoy!
